Our Story

Our Story

Simplify Local was born from a simple observation: Irish small business owners were spending more time on admin than on their actual craft.

Founded in Mullingar by a team with many years of experience in both technology and small business operations, we noticed a pattern. Whether it was personal trainers playing phone tag, barbers managing bookings in notebooks, or dog groomers tracking vaccinations on paper scraps – the story was the same.

We realized that while complex enterprise solutions existed, they were often over-engineered and over-priced for local businesses. What was missing was simple, affordable automation designed specifically for the Irish market.

Today, we've helped many Irish businesses automate their operations, reclaiming thousands of hours and significantly boosting their revenue.
